# RefPip Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 7, 2026**

RefPip (the "Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

## 1. Data Collection
RefPip **does not collect, store, or transmit** any of your personal data, browsing history, or AI chat content to external servers.

## 2. Local Processing
All data processing (cloning chat blocks, creating floating portals, and mirroring content) happens **locally on your machine** within your browser's memory and local storage.

## 3. Storage
RefPip uses `chrome.storage.sync` to save your preferences (such as Selection Mode, Auto-inject, and Portal Theme). This data is synced across your Chrome instances via your Google account but is never accessed by the RefPip developers.

## 4. Third-Party AI Sites
RefPip interacts with AI chat interfaces (like Gemini, AI Studio, and ChatGPT) by reading their DOM structure to inject "Pin" buttons. We do not access your account credentials or private messages.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

## 6. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the [Contact Form](https://refpip.com/#contact) or on our [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/roypatel1/refpip).
